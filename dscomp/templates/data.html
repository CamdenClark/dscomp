Competition Data

Get Started

To join the UI Data Science Competition:

  1. Create an account using your UI email address, and confirm your email.
  2. Download the competition data sets from this page.
  3. Check out the details about competition scoring.
  4. Upload submissions between March 12th and April 22nd.

The entire UI community is welcome to participate in either or both of the competitions. Early and multiple submissions are encouraged in both competitions--the first 80 students with a complete submission will earn a Data Science Competition T-shirt!

Be sure to join the UI DS Comp Slack community for announcements, examples, and data science discussion.

Good luck!
